The Swarm

This Unity3D project contains a bullet hell type of game. Players fight against incoming enemy waves with their characters that they control from a top-down perspective. As players level up, they have the chance to choose from customizable upgrade options or new weapons to enhance their characters. The project includes various enemy movement patterns, different weapon types and strategic options.
